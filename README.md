# Alliance of Muslim Women in Spirituality and Humanity 

About Alliance of Muslim Women in Spirituality and Humanity
⁠"Cultivating The Beauty of Faith"

𝐖𝐇𝐎 𝐈𝐒 𝐀𝐌𝐖𝐨𝐒𝐇?
The Alliance of Muslim Women in Spirituality and Humanity (AMWoSH), established in 2022, champions the holistic growth of Muslim Women through education, community, and advocacy initiatives. Rooted in our commitment to Islam, Women, and Peace as our focus areas, we ensure that our sisters’ spiritual journey is at the heart of our efforts, providing them with the opportunities to contribute meaningfully to the community.

𝐕𝐈𝐒𝐈𝐎𝐍
AMWoSH envisions a sisterhood that cultivates the beauty of Islamic spirituality, embodies merciful love rooted in the Sunnah, and carries this mercy to inspire humanity through mawaddah.


𝐌𝐈𝐒𝐒𝐈𝐎𝐍
AMWoSH aims to provide a space for Muslim Women to deepen their spiritual journey by nurturing sisterhood, serving the community, and supporting women in achieving their full potential through learning and shared purpose, with a focus on Islam, Women, and Peace.
